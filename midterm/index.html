The story of OCTANE

Don Toliver's album OCTANE (released January 30, 2026, as his fifth studio album) centers on a high-energy, motorhead-inspired concept tied to his lifelong passion for cars, driving, and speed. The title "Octane" refers to high-octane fuel, symbolizing revved-up energy, momentum, fast living, and pushing limits—both sonically and thematically.
